***Welcome to the realm where mathematical elegance meets practical innovation! I am Kristina, a passionate explorer of the intricate landscapes of Applied Mathematics*** 🤗.

## Academic Journey:
In June 2020, I proudly attained my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from the Department of Applied Mathematics at <a href="https://www.cycu.edu.tw"> Chung Yuan Christian University (CYCU)</a>, Taiwan, marking the culmination of years of dedicated study and research. Prior to this achievement, I earned both my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Mathematics from the prestigious <a href="https://www.usu.ac.id/en"> University of Sumatera Utara</a>, Indonesia. These formative years laid the foundation for my relentless pursuit of mathematical excellence.


## Professional Endeavors:
Following my academic achievements, I embarked on a mission to inspire future generations of mathematicians. In November 2020, I returned to Indonesia to impart knowledge and wisdom as a faculty member at <a href="https://binus.ac.id">BINUS University</a>'s graduate program. This experience fueled my passion for teaching and research, propelling me to pursue a transformative postdoctoral position at <a href="https://www.cycu.edu.tw">CYCU</a>'s Department of Applied Mathematics in March 2023.

## Research Focus:
My research journey is guided by an insatiable curiosity to unravel the mysteries of **unsupervised learning (clustering)** and **pattern recognition**. At the heart of my endeavors lies a commitment to pushing the boundaries of knowledge and innovation. Through rigorous exploration and experimentation, I develop novel unsupervised learning algorithms tailored for both single-view and multi-view data. Notably, during my role as a postdoctoral fellow at CYCU, I successfully developed new **federated learning** techniques for multi-view clustering, addressing the challenge of handling multiple clients’ multi-view data using **innovative objective functions**.


## Philosophy:
I believe that mathematics is not merely a tool but a form of art—a manifestation of beauty and elegance. In my teaching, I strive to instill this appreciation for beauty, recognizing its power to transcend disciplines and inspire creativity. Collaboration and communication are integral to my research philosophy, as I actively seek to foster dialogue within the academic community.
